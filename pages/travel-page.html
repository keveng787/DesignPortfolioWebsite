For this travel app project, I was tasked to design a service aimed at young, adventurous travelers, primarily college students and recent graduates. The project began with in-depth research into competitors like Lonely Planet, Footprint, and Letâ€™s Go, to understand how to appeal to a budget-conscious, experience-focused audience. I created over a dozen initial sketches and a wireframe concentrating on user-impact, ease of use, and ease of navigation. I then created a moodboard to help guide the design aesthetics. From there, I developed an interactive prototype featuring a clean navigation interface, over ten interactive pages, and multimedia content such as videos and images. The prototype features many interactive elements and animations using custom Figma components and variables. I culminated the project with a pitch, emphasizing the app's ease of use, alignment with the brand, and its appeal to a younger audience seeking memorable, off-the-beaten-path adventures.
