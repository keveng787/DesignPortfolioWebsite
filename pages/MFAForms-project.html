As a designer for Sandbox, Northeastern's student-led software consultancy, I designed and delivered MFAForms, a tool created to streamline form management at the Museum of Fine Arts in Boston. Joining the project mid-way through, I worked with in team of 7 developers and 2 designers to deliver the tool on-time to the client.

I was responsible for designing some of the final components, screens, and flows to help get the project over the finish line!
