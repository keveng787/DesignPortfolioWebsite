For this short project, I was tasked to create a digital interpretation of a physical interface. The translation was a interpretation physical interface that helps you accomplish the same goal and, ideally, the same task through, what is most likely, different steps. I decided to use a record player for my object and created an app to interpret the object.

Sketches

I started with some quick sketches to outline the digital interactions for the record player app. I then went through usability audits to test the interactions and overall user experience of the app.
